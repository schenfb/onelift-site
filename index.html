How It Works

1

Pick up the dumbbell

Five exercises, one per day, rotating. Bulgarian split squat, single-arm press, single-leg deadlift, single-arm row, single-arm bench press.

2

Do 5 reps, 3 sets per side

Weak side first. Same weight for all sets. Rest 2-3 minutes between sets. The whole thing takes about 20 minutes.

3

Add a little weight next time

The app suggests your next weight. You confirm based on what your dumbbell actually has. Small increments, consistently, over months.

4

Hit a wall? Deload and rebuild

When it gets too heavy, step back 4 weeks of progress and climb again. You'll pass your old number and keep going. This is the cycle: load, deload, reload.
